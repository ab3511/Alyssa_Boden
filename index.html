A little about me.

BACKGROUND

I grew up in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, an avid Steelers and Penguins fan. Upon graduation from high school, I attended the University of Richmond in Virginia to study Business Administration and Political Science. From there, I moved to Nashville, TN to work in Market Research at the Country Music Association and the healthcare industry. After falling in love with research and data analysis, I wanted to get more involved with the implementation and delivery of strategic decisions identified through research. I then moved to Washington, D.C. to work at Accenture and have been there for the last five years supporting technology implementation initiatives for the federal government. At the same time I have been attending the Georgetown McDonough School of Business in pursuit of my MBA. Expected graduation is May 2020.
